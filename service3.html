Dog Grooming

At Paws and Play Dog Services, we believe that every dog deserves to look and feel their absolute best. Our dog grooming service is more than just a trim; it's a pampering session designed to enhance your furry friend's well-being. Our team of professional groomers is dedicated to providing top-notch care, offering a range of services including baths, haircuts, nail trims, and more. We understand that each dog is unique, and our grooming sessions are tailored to cater to their specific coat type, breed, and individual needs.

Imagine your dog enjoying a luxurious spa day at Paws and Play. Our grooming sessions go beyond aesthetics - they are designed to promote your dog's health and happiness. From soothing baths that cleanse their coat and skin to stylish haircuts that suit their breed, our groomers pay meticulous attention to detail. Nail trims, ear cleaning, and other grooming essentials are executed with care and expertise, ensuring your dog not only looks fabulous but also feels comfortable and relaxed.
