About us

Discover Unparalleled Pet Care at Paws & Play Dog Services:

Welcome to Paws & Play Dog Services, where your furry companions are not just pets but cherished members of our extended family. As your dedicated partner in pet care, we take pride in offering a comprehensive suite of services crafted to meet the unique needs of your beloved dogs. From invigorating dog walks and pampering grooming sessions to comfortable boarding and specialized training, we are committed to ensuring your pets thrive in an environment filled with love, activity, and personalized attention. Your pet's well-being is not just our mission; it's our unwavering priority.

"We're Paws & Play - Your dog's best friend. We offer top-quality services to keep your furry companion happy and healthy."

"We offer a comprehensive range of services for dogs, including dog walking, grooming, boarding, training, and more. Our goal is to provide everything your canine companion needs for a happy, healthy life."

"Our goal is to ensure the well-being and happiness of your dogs. We aim to be your trusted partner, providing expert care, love, and support for your furry family members."

"What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to your dog's well-being. We combine expertise, love, and a tailored approach to ensure every dog's unique needs are met."

"Choose our services because we are passionate dog lovers with a proven track record of delivering top-quality care. Your dog's happiness and health are our highest priorities."

We are fully qualified, licensed and insured.
